The theme for the Global Game Jam 2017 was waves and because it was my first GameJam, I decided to keep it simple. I partecipated with a friend and we had a lot of fun!

Our idea was to make a Layton style puzzle game with a seaside theme and use the movement of waves to solve it. We used Unity with scripts in C# and I used Pixelmator to draw the assets.

Here's how you play it. By pressing the directional arrows you move all the movable pieces on the grid by 1 step in the direction you chose. Your goal is to place all the pearls back to the corresponding shell.

After the 48 hours we eneded up with the two working levels that you can see below.
